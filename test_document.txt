SECTION 11: TROUBLESHOOTING FAQ
==================================================

Q:
What should I do if Kafka is unavailable?

Answer:
Check broker health, verify network connectivity,
restart consumers if necessary, and inspect topic
partitions.

----------------------------------

Q:
What should I do if embeddings are missing?

Answer:
Re-run the embedding pipeline,
verify EmbedX-1024 service health,
confirm vectors exist in Qdrant.

----------------------------------

Q:
How do I reset MFA?

Answer:
Open an IT ticket and verify identity with HR.
